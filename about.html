About CIFROT

Who am I?

My name is Jah-On (John Schulz) and I have bilateral Cochlear Implants. I've had Cochlear Implants for most of my life. Presently, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering and have been programming for nearly 5 years.


Why did I make CIFROT?

I am sensitive about how my Cochlear Implants sound and I notice that I get more headaches when they sound off. My audiologist has been excellent but sometimes its hard to describe what I'm hearing. I wanted to make a tool that would allow me to test my Cochlear Implant's frequency response and save the results for my audiologist. I hope this software will help other Cochlear Implant users as well.
